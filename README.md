This repository contains the relevant notebooks used to generate figures in the manuscript:

Emma Kuan, Nicholas Moss, Catalina Sakai et al. Dissecting type I and II interferon impacts on human immune cells in disease by a cell type-specific interferon response atlas, 23 December 2025, PREPRINT (Version 1) available at [Research Square](https://www.researchsquare.com/article/rs-8197575/v1)


## Figures
### Main
**IFN_Fig1.ipynb** Experimental design and transcriptomic responses to four different IFNs in 13 immune subsets.

**IFN_Fig2.ipynb** Transcriptomic features of 13 immune cell subsets in response to IFN-α, IFN-β and IFN-λ1.

**IFN_Fig3.ipynb** Transcriptomic features of 13 immune cell subsets in responding to IFN-γ and its comparison to IFN-α.

**IFN_Fig4.ipynb** Dynamics of systemic IFNs and their responses in immune cells in NDMM.

**IFN_Fig5.ipynb** Application of HIRISA to multiple human immune disease cohorts. 

### Supp

**IFN_Sup1.ipynb** Differential IFN responses in IFN receptor genes in different immune subsets.

**IFN_Sup2.ipynb** Comparison of enriched versus PBMC IFN-α response.

**IFN_Sup3.ipynb** Transcriptomic features of 13 immune cell subsets in response to IFN-α, IFN-β and IFN-λ1

**IFN_Sup4.ipynb** Transcriptomic features of 13 immune cell subsets in response to IFN-γ.

**IFN_Sup5.ipynb** IFN-α and IFN-γ scores in NDMM.

**IFN_Sup6.ipynb** IFN-α and IFN-γ scores in patients with myeloma or infection.

## Scripts

**IFN_Score_Generate.R** Function to generate donor-level IFN response scores

**IFNa_IFNg_Scoring_ISGs_Mat.csv** Input ISG response matrix for IFN scoring function
